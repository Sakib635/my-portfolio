## 2025

- **Understanding the Popularity of Packages in Maven Ecosystem**  
  Sadman Jashim Sakib, Muhammad Asaduzzaman, Curtis Bright, Cole Morgan  
  *2025 IEEE/ACM 22nd International Conference on Mining Software Repositories (MSR), Ottawa, Canada, pp. 364–368*  
  Mining Challenge Track  
  [IEEE Xplore](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/11025736) · [DOI: 10.1109/MSR66628.2025.00067](https://doi.org/10.1109/MSR66628.2025.00067) · [Preprint (PDF)](https://www.preprints.org/manuscript/202504.2296/v1) · [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/392668987_Understanding_the_Popularity_of_Packages_in_Maven_Ecosystem)

  Large-scale empirical study analyzing 103,315 Maven packages (≥2 years old) to uncover drivers of package popularity, adoption patterns, growth/peak/decay trends, and implications for ecosystem health and dependency evaluation.

- **Toward Better Dependency Management in Python Projects**  
  Sadman Jashim Sakib  
  *Electronic Theses and Dissertations 9776, University of Windsor, 2025*  
  M.Sc. Thesis (Defended: May 9, 2025)  
  [UWindsor Scholarship](https://scholar.uwindsor.ca/etd/9776) · [Direct PDF](https://uwindsor.scholaris.ca/bitstreams/139be672-4d20-4801-a358-1af02a068f3f/download) · [Mirror PDF (group site)](https://cs.uwaterloo.ca/~cbright/group/sadman_thesis.pdf)

  Proposed SMT-based techniques (e.g., SMTpip prototype) to resolve version conflicts and handle missing/incomplete configuration files in Python dependency ecosystems.
